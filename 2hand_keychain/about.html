About Key2hand

The organization behind the shop — based in Da Nang City, Vietnam

Key2hand is a Da Nang City–based organization passionate about giving pre-loved keychains a second life. Founded by a small team of collectors and keychain enthusiasts right here in Da Nang, we believe every charm has more story left to tell.

We source, inspect, and fairly price second-hand keychains — anime, vintage, handcrafted, and more — so that unique pieces stay out of landfills and find people who will truly treasure them. No bulk imports, no dropshipping; only real items with real histories.

Our mission is to connect keychain lovers across Vietnam with affordable, authentic pre-owned pieces, while building a community around sustainable collecting right here in Da Nang. If it carries a story, it belongs here. 💜

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I order? +
Browse the shop, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Our team will contact you on Zalo within 24 hours to confirm and arrange delivery.
How accurate are the condition descriptions? +
"Like New" means never used. "Good" has light signs of use. "Fair" has visible wear but remains charming and functional. We price fairly for each condition.
Do you ship outside Vietnam? +
We currently ship within Vietnam only. International shipping may be available for bulk orders — contact us to discuss.
Can I return an item? +
If an item differs significantly from its description, contact us within 3 days of receiving it. We will work out a fair solution.
Can I request a specific keychain? +
Sometimes! Message us with what you're looking for. Our team regularly visits flea markets and conventions across Da Nang and beyond.
